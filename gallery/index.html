Gallery

This is a small collection of  Still Life  and animals that I painted with acrylics. You can click on any of the images below to see them in maximum resolution.

All paintings were made with four colors:  Cadmium Red ,  Cadmium Yellow ,  Ultramarine Blue , and  Titanium White,  which I mix by hand.

I'd like to add a  portrait  to the collection at some point. For now, some of the paintings I like the most decorate the walls in my home.
